1. Scope of This Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how GymMax (“we,” “our,” or “us”) collects, uses, discloses, and protects information when you install or use the GymMax mobile application or any related services (collectively, the “Service”).

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Core Functionality – Analyze images, generate scores and workout plans, and deliver one (1) new AI scan per subscription week.
  • Account Services – Authenticate you, remember preferences, and provide customer support.
  • Analytics & Improvement – Monitor performance, fix bugs, and refine our AI models (data used in aggregate or de‑identified form where feasible).
  • Legal & Security – Detect fraud, enforce Terms of Service, and comply with applicable laws.

4. Retention & Deletion

Data Type Retention Period
Physique photos Deleted or fully anonymized within 30 days of the associated AI scan, unless you trigger an earlier deletion in‑app.

Account & subscription records Retained for as long as necessary to administer the Service and meet legal or accounting obligations.

You may delete your account (and associated data) at any time within the app settings. Deletion requests are processed within 30 days, subject to legal holds.

All third parties are bound by confidentiality and data‑processing agreements.

6. Security

We implement industry-standard safeguards, including:

  • Encryption in transit and at rest for image files and personal data.
  • Strict access controls – data accessible only to personnel on a need-to-know basis.
  • Routine security audits and vulnerability testing.

No system is 100% secure; you use the Service at your own risk.
